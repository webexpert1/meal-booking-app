EAT. DRINK. GIVE.

Meals on Wheels is an application that allows customers to make food orders and helps the food caterers know what the customers want to eat. Meals on Wheels is a mobile app and website that provides recipe recommendations personalized to the individual's tastes, semantic recipe search, a digital recipe box, shopping list and one-hour grocery delivery.

OUR HISTORY

Craft meals, for some time, was an exclusive culture; it existed in the basements of food lovers, on niche blogs, and in obscure, tucked away restaurants. For outsiders, craft meals can be intimidating. In fact, we’re still learning. After all, it’s a big foodhouse out there. We believes that craft meals should be accessible to everyone. In Lagos 2019, we saw an opportunity to bring craft meals to the people.
